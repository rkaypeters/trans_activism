Trans Rights are Human Rights

Anti-trans legislation and Twitter resilience

Liz Cory & Rachel Peterson, Spring 2021

Anti-transgender legislation is on the rise.

Only three months into 2021, US state legislators have introduced an historic number of new bills that threaten the rights of transgender people — 93, as of this writing. During the 2020 session, a staggering 79 anti-transgender bills were filed, the previous record-high.

Several of the bills in the current session specifically target trans children, who would be barred from accessing gender-affirming healthcare and participating in athletics with their cisgender peers. Even in areas where these bills are unlikely to pass, just introducing them is harmful to trans people, especially children who are greater social and emotional risk than their peers.

This wave of discriminatory policy has ignited public discourse and calls for action have resounded on Twitter under the hashtag #TransRightsAreHumanRights.

But trans folks are showing resilience nationwide.
